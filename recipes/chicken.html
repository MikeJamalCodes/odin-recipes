When it comes to chicken there just isnt anything more delicious than a juicy, crusty piece of finger-licking good fried chicken. It might seem intimidating to fry your own chicken, but its actually pretty straightforward and it puts grocery store and fast food fried chicken to shame.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Mix indgredients in large bowl
  2. Heat Vegetable oil to 350 degrees
  3. Work one at a time, place chicken in fryer
