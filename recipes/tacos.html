Pulled Chicken Tacos

Pulled chicken tacos are an easy, stress-free weeknight meal that doesn't sacrifice flavor for convenience. Whether you have chicken breasts or chicken thighs in your fridge, this simple recipe can be made with either.

To increase the versatility, we've instructions for preparing the chicken in an Instant Pot (or another electric pressure cooker) or a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Small Onion, quartered
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • Mexican Seasonings to taste

Steps

  1. Season the Meat
  2. Add chicken to skillet, bring to simmer
  3. Shred meat with a fork
  4. Add seasoning to the meat
  5. Use whatever toppings you like
